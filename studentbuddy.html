Student Buddy


Reinvented student customer support that goes above and beyond for your tenants.

Each new tenant’s questions take on average 8 minutes of your staff’s time during the arrival and departure periods..

We can help you save this time, save resources, and get more done.

Expensive hours are used to answer even basic questions when tenants first move in and as a room provider, you naturally want to answer all of them. However, this means your skilled employees are prevented from doing valuable work

Our solution


  • The digital buddy
    At Starter Kit, our team of international students called Buddies are ready at all times to chat with your tenants, helping with any questions. Having similar profiles allows for fluid conversations and sky-high satisfaction.
  • More versatile buddies
    The more information you provide to our knowledge database, the further it decreases the load in your inbox as our Buddies becomes more versatile.
  • Transparency
    Want to know what each of your tenants asked our buddies? All customer service tickets are shared to you with our intuitive system so that you can easily find information about tenants and gather more relevant information about your tenants.
