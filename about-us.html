About us


By students for students

In early 2018, our founders created Starter Kit with the goal to improve the experience of students going to university. Since then we have been working with housing organizations, universities and student associations across the Netherlands to help students move in comfy.
Starter Kit is now providing Starter Kits in all major student cities across the Netherlands.

Making students move in comfy is what keeps us awake at night!

We also believe that no one understands international students better than students, that is why nearly everyone in the team has been or currently is an international student.
