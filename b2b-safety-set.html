What's in the set?

10 Disposable Face mask EN 14683 - 3 Ply

The EN 14683 is a standard for respiratory equipment which filters 95% of particulates greater than 0.3 microns. EN 14683 masks are a PPE.

These masks are compliants with:
ISO 13485:2016
ISO 45001:2018
ISO 10002



1 Bottle of Hand Sanitizer (100ml)

This antimicrobial gel contains 70 percent ethyl alcohol to reduce by 99.9% the amount of germs and microbs.

Each kit comes in a sealed plastic bag for maximum hygiene.
